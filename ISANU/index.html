ABOUT

ISANU (Indonesian Students Association at Northeastern University) is a student-led cultural organization with the purpose of uniting the Indonesian community within the university as well as to promote the Indonesian culture with the community in the Greater Boston Area.Our events set the stage to build a close-knit community for our fellow Indonesian students as well as provide a home-away-from home. We engage in different collaborative projects with other associations within and outside Northeastern University to provide our members with opportunities to get involved with wider communities and meet new people.

The Indonesian Students Association at Northeastern University serves as a gateway for all students who have an interest in the culture and aesthetic of Indonesia. With our focus on cultivating a developed Indonesian community in Boston University, ISANU aims to connect every individual on campus and provide a valuable and cultural learning experience.

September 29

INDOFEST 2019: The New England Indonesian Festival is back for the sixth year running! This year's theme is “Going Local!”, which aims to showcase and celebrate the daily lives and customs of local Indonesians. By offering a glimpse of our rich culture, we hope to give all of our NEIF visitors a personal taste of Indonesia at its roots and transport them to the small kampungs of tropical Indonesia. Our team is working hard to bring you a remarkable IndoFest experience filled with food, dances, music and more entertainment! Please save the date and witness the lively tropical Indonesian spirit yourself at #IndoFest18.
